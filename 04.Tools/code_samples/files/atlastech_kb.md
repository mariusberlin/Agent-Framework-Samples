## 4. HR Policies

### Leave Entitlement
- **Annual leave:** 18 working days per year (accrued monthly)
- **Public holidays:** 11 days (Moroccan national calendar)
- **Sick leave:** Up to 6 days/year without medical certificate; unlimited with certificate
- **Maternity leave:** 14 weeks (fully paid)
- **Paternity leave:** 15 calendar days

### Remote Work Policy
- Eligible after **6-month probation period**
- Maximum **3 days remote per week**
- Must be physically present for client meetings and all-hands days (first Monday of each month)
- Remote work abroad requires prior HR approval (max 30 days/year)

### Expense Reimbursement
- Submit expense reports within **5 business days** of the trip
- Per diem (domestic Morocco): **600 MAD/day** (meals + incidentals)
- Per diem (international): **1,800 MAD/day**
- Hotel bookings: use preferred vendor list (see intranet) or max 800 MAD/night in Casablanca

## 5. IT & Internal Procedures

### Equipment Requests
- Submit via internal portal: **portal.atlastech.internal**
- Lead time: minimum **2 weeks** from approval
- Approval required from direct manager (budget < 5,000 MAD) or department head (budget ≥ 5,000 MAD)
- Standard laptop refresh cycle: **every 3 years**

### Software Licensing
- All software must be approved by IT before installation — submit request to **it@atlastech.ma**
- Unapproved software installations will trigger a security audit
- Licensed software list available on the intranet wiki

### Network & VPN
- VPN client: **AtlasVPN v3.2** (download from portal.atlastech.internal/vpn)
- VPN is mandatory when accessing internal systems remotely
- Report connectivity issues to **helpdesk@atlastech.ma** with subject line: `[VPN] <issue>`
